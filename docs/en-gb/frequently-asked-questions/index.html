Automation Center of Excellence

Question: How is the Automation Kit for Power Platform related to the CoE Kit?

They are complimentary solutions that your Center of Excellence team may install to help manage your Low Code Environment.

Question: Why is the Automation Kit for Power Platform not a another modules of the CoE Kit?

The Automation Kit for Power Platform has a focused on the Automation Lifecycle. As indicated in the Automation Center Of Excellence each of the kits meet the needs of different components and activities of the overall process.

Question: Can the Automation Kit for Power Platform and CoE Kit be used together?

Absolutely, the ALM Accelerator within the CoE Kit is one example where Power Automate Desktop deployment and integrate with the Automation Kit for Power Platform are a natural combination to automate integration with source control and automate deployment between environments.
