Getting further support

Guidance on getting further support for the Automation Kit for Power Platform:

  • Refer to our Feedback and support page for general guidance

  • Use our Automation Kit Learn pages to step through the install and usage instructions

  • Use the Automation Kit Issues templates for search for known Bugs / Feature gaps. If you do not find a match for your issue look to create a new issue using one of the supplied templates

  • Use our [walkthrough videos](n each milestone due to priority bugs or features) to assist you with show how installation process works

  • Join our Office Hours to ask questions on common issues or product roadmap

  • Contact your Microsoft Global Black Belt, Cloud Solution Architect or normal Support Channels for further guidance.

Power CAT Team

The core Power CAT team are unable to do custom troubleshooting or consulting to a broader audience. If you are experiencing issues with or have questions about the Automation Kit for Power Platform, Join our Office Hours or instead raise your issue or questions via https://aka.ms/ak4pp-issues
